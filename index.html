About Me

I'm Atti Cindy Lynnette, a Data Science student at Uganda Christian University in Mukono, with a deep passion for using technology and data to create meaningful impact in the world.

My interests span business intelligence, finance technology, analytics, and AI — areas where I believe data has the power to transform communities, organisations, and lives. I approach every challenge with curiosity, discipline, and a drive to grow.

"I believe growth, creativity, and bringing out the best in the people around me are the most powerful tools I have."

Beyond data, I'm a creative soul — I love modelling, fashion, music, and design. My long-term vision is to become a world-class data scientist working with leading global technology and finance companies, while inspiring others to pursue their own ambitions in tech.
